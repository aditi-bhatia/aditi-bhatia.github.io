HippoTime and PorkiBall: Human-Computer-Interaction Technology

Winner: UCI Multidisciplinary Design Program Research Grant (2014, 2015, 2016)

Featured in Interface Magazine â€” Spring 2015

HippoTime is an animated video game which uses biofeedback from the user to play and navigate the game (Levels of the game pictured above). The goal of this product is to equip children with ASD with the proper tools for emotion regulation. Use cases of this game include tantrum intervention, breathing therapy training, and effectiveness in clinical and home setting.

Similarly, PorkiBall is aimed at helping increase social interactions between parents and children with ASD through the innovative toy. PorkiBall is aimed at being cost-effective, effective for home setting, and requires no special training for intervention.
