Aditi's Projects Lullabyte



Lullabyte: An IoT Android Application

An app to improve storytime between parent and child. Transforms a spoken story into an animation of relevant images, and changes mood lighting to match the emotions of the story.

A child’s development is heavily impacted by the amount of time and the quality of time spent with their parents. Studies have shown that parents engaged more with their children with the help of technology. The goal of this project is to improve interaction during storytelling by adding “smart” features for higher engagement between the parent and child.

A traditional story book is defined, static, and has minimal interactions. Lullabyte is creative, dynamic, interactive, and promotes bonding.

Lullabyte can also be used for assisting children with learning difficulties, children with autism + difficulties with understanding emotions, and children having difficulties with focusing.


Features:
Speech Recognition (Google Speech-To-Text)

Natural Language Understanding (IBM Watson Speech Analytics)

Internet of Things (Philips Hue Lightbulb API)

Visual Representations (Giphy API)
